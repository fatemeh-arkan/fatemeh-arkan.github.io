About

I am an MS/Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) under the supervision of Dr. Nader Sehatbakhsh. Currently, I am a researcher in the Secure Systems and Architectures Lab (Ssysarch).

I received my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology in 2021. For my final project, I was working on Cloud Platforms in Next-Generation Mobile Networks as an undergrad researcher.
